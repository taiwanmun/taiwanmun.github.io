What is Tawan Model United Nations?

Model United Nations

Today the conference, tomorrow the world.

Model United Nations is a student conference with the aim of bringing the negotiations and experience of a real UN conference to life. Academically we not only aim to simulate the United Nations and grant our delegates the opportunity to expand their global views and leadership talents but also cultivate a new generation of young innovators ready to become the present of the future.

"Taiwan" Model United Nations

We came, we saw, we TWMUN.

TWMUN is co-founded by National Chiao Tung University MUN and National Tsing Hua University MUN, and is also meant to symbolize a close cooperation between the two schools in the realm of international affairs. Undergraduate and graduate students gather for three days to discuss, debate, and to solve a variety of current global and local issues. TWMUN brings annually special committees to expand the learning horizons of our delegates.
